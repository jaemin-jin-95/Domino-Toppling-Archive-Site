About TheDominoSwag Jin

I created my channel on October 27, 2007. At that time, I was only watching a bunch of domino videos. I found a FlippyCat's channel for my inspiration. So, I began to upload my domino videos and started the classic domino series. Although I had few subscribers back then, I was not thinking about becoming famous on my channel.
